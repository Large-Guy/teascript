# Overloading

## Operators

- equals
```tea
== (a, b)
```
Overloading of this operator also overloads `!=`

- add
```tea
+ (a, b)
```

- subtract / negate
```tea
- (a, b)
```

- multiply
```tea
* (a, b)
```

- divide
```tea
/ (a, b)
```

- mod
```tea
% (a, b)
```

- pow
```tea
** (a, b)
```

- band
```tea
& (a, b)
```

- bor
```tea
| (a, b)
```

- bnot
```tea
~ (a, b)
```

- bxor
```tea
^ (a, b)
```

- lshift
```tea
<< (a, b)
```

- rshift
```tea
>> (a, b)
```

- and
- or

- greater
```tea
> (a, b)
```

- less
```tea
< (a, b)
```

- subscript
```tea
[] (index, value)
```

typeof()
